Fivemanage is an all-in-one SaaS platform for communities looking to improve their workflow, offering a range of features to faciliate easier management
and administration of your FiveM server. They offer fast and reliable content delivery - with full support for image, video, and audio files - and seamless
logging to track player and server activity while ensuring dependable performance and security.

Fivemanage integrates seamlessly into your FiveM server with native support via ox\_lib and oxmysql, or through their official FiveM SDK.

They offer a generous free plan with enough storage to get you started, and a variety of paid plans designed for both small and large servers needing access to more storage and advanced feeatures.

Get started with [Fivemanage](https://refer.fivemanage.com/overextended) today.
